About Rise & Strike

Training That Comes to You

Rise & Strike is South Wales's mobile personal training company built on a simple belief: fitness should be fun, functional, and a little bit fierce.

With a decade of experience — including 5 years training clients in Kuwait — and a lifetime on the pitch and in the gym, we bring real-world knowledge and a seriously good time to every session. No commute, no gym anxiety. Just you, your trainer, and a whole lot of progress.

Our holistic approach blends the discipline of boxing and self-defence with evidence-based corrective exercise and practical conditioning. You'll leave every session feeling stronger, more capable, and genuinely buzzing.
