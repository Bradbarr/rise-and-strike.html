About Rise & Strike

Training That Comes to You

Rise & Strike is South Wales's mobile personal training service built on one belief: the right training, done well, changes everything — at any age, at any stage.

With a decade of experience — including 5 years training clients in Kuwait — and qualifications in corrective exercise, weight loss, and personal training from NASM, every session is thoughtfully built around your body, your goals, and your life.

No gym needed. No prior fitness experience required. Whether you're recovering from an injury, managing pain, building strength for the first time, or looking to level up your performance — Rise & Strike meets you where you are.
